Ancestry

The Rizal family had Chinese, Japanese, and Filipino ancestry. Their original surname was Mercado, but they adopted Rizal in 1731 to avoid Spanish persecution.

The family belonged to the principalia (noble class) and were well-respected landowners in Calamba, Laguna.
