Module 3: The Genealogy and Education of Our Hero

Lesson 1: Family Background and Heritage

The Mercado-Rizal Family

José Protasio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda was born on June 19, 1861, in Calamba, Laguna. His family belonged to the principalia (noble class) and were well-respected landowners in Calamba, Laguna.

Family Heritage

  • Ancestry
    • Chinese heritage through both parents
    • Japanese ancestry traced through family lineage
    • Filipino roots deeply embedded in Laguna
    • Mixed cultural influences shaped his worldview
  • Family Name History
    • Original surname: Mercado
    • Changed to Rizal in 1731 to avoid Spanish persecution
    • Name "Rizal" derived from "Ricial" (green fields)
    • Family maintained both names through generations

Lesson 2: Early Education

First Teachers

  • Mother's Influence
    • Teodora Alonso - his first teacher
    • Taught him to read and write by age 5
    • Instilled love for learning and literature
  • Private Tutors
    • Maestro Celestino - taught Latin
    • Maestro Lucas Padua - taught art
    • José Alberto - taught surveying and Spanish

Early Academic Achievements

  • Learned to read and write at an early age
  • Showed talent in poetry and art
  • Mastered multiple languages
  • Excelled in academic subjects
