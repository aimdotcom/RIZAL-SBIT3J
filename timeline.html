Timeline of Jose Rizal's Life

1861

Birth and Early Years

Born José Protasio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda on June 19 in Calamba, Laguna. Seventh child of Francisco Mercado and Teodora Alonso, a prosperous farming family of mixed Chinese, Japanese, and Filipino ancestry.

1869-1871

Early Education

  • Learned reading and writing from his mother by age 5
  • Wrote first poem "Sa Aking Mga Kabata" at age 8
  • Studied under private tutors:
    • Maestro Celestino (Latin)
    • Maestro Lucas Padua (Art)
    • José Alberto (Surveying and Spanish)

1872-1877

Ateneo Municipal de Manila

  • Enrolled in 1872
  • Excelled in:
    • Philosophy and Letters
    • Science and Mathematics
    • Languages and Literature
    • Art and Drawing
  • Graduated with highest honors

1877-1882

University of Santo Tomas

  • Studied medicine and philosophy
  • Faced discrimination but persevered
  • Continued literary pursuits
  • Decided to continue studies in Spain

1882-1885

First Travel Abroad

  • Spain (Madrid):
    • Studied at Universidad Central de Madrid
    • Earned Licentiate in Medicine
    • Completed Philosophy and Letters degree
  • France (Paris):
    • Studied art under renowned artists
    • Observed European culture
  • Germany (Heidelberg):
    • Studied ophthalmology under Dr. Otto Becker
    • Learned German language

1887

First Homecoming and Noli Me Tangere

  • Published "Noli Me Tangere" in Berlin
  • Returned to Philippines
  • Practiced medicine in Calamba
  • Treated his mother's failing eyesight
  • Faced growing opposition from Spanish authorities

1888-1891

Second Travel Abroad

  • Traveled through:
    • Japan (Tokyo, Yokohama)
    • United States (San Francisco)
    • United Kingdom (London)
    • France (Paris)
    • Belgium (Brussels, Ghent)
  • Published "El Filibusterismo" (1891)
  • Annotated Morga's "Sucesos"
  • Wrote for La Solidaridad

1892-1896

Return, Exile, and Final Years

  • 1892: Established La Liga Filipina
  • Exiled to Dapitan:
    • Established a school for boys
    • Created water system for town
    • Practiced medicine
    • Met Josephine Bracken

December 1896

Trial and Execution

  • December 26-29: Court martial trial
  • December 29: Found guilty of rebellion
  • December 30:
    • Wrote "Mi Último Adiós"
    • Married Josephine Bracken
    • Executed at Bagumbayan at 7:03 AM
    • Last words: "consummatum est"
